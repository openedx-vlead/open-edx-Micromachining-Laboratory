For the purpose of machining, Electra-Ecocut 4-axis wireEDM with brass wire of diameter 300μm was used with deionized water as dielectric. It employs a MOSFET type pulse generator with three chief controlling parameters viz. Ton, Toff, Toff1. Appropriate machining operation is achieved with the help of adequate wire feed control and dielectric flushing rate. Surface roughness of machined component was measured using Taylor-Hobson surface roughness tester (Model: Surtronic-25). Weight of the material was taken on Afcoset Electronic Balance (Model: FX-400). Tektronix TDS2012 model digital oscilloscope was connected to the machine to measure number of pulses.
