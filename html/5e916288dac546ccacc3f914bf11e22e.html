In EMG process.

1.    Material removal rate increases with increase in current density.

2.    Material removal rate for materials with lower valency is higher than materials with higher valency for similar current density values.

3.    The current density values are significantly affected by input voltage and electrolyte concentration used, which again affects the material removal rate

4.    As oxidized surface of workpiece is removed by grinding wheel. This results in much less distortion of workpiece than mechanical grinding.
