The Conclusions deviveried from the experiment are summarized as below:

1.    The process parameters like beam power and beam spot size as well as the thermo physical properties of the material affect the process independently.

2.    The penetration depth and melt area of laser spot weld are found to be complicated function of laser pulse energy, intensity and beam diameter.

3.    De-focussed laser beam diversifies the beam thus increases the weld pool diameter but decreases the penetration depth. This also reduces weld strength.

4.    The observation of the microstructure of the individual spot at cross section shows a homogeneous and continuous built layer without the presence of any macro cracks.
