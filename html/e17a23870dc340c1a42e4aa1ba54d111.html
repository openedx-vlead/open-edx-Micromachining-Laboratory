The conclusions derived form the experiment are summarized as below:

1.    In modelling of thermal processing for materials pulsed heating models are appropriate for any manufacturing operations

2.    The energy of pulse drives/dominates the machining process viz. heat treatment, welding erosion and cutting/machining.

3.    Pulsed heating model is idle for intermittent cutting like EDM/WEDM.

4.    Pulsed modeling can be simulated for continuous wave with changes in idle time (minimization).
