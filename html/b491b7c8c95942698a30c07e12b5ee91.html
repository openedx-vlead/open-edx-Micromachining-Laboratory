The conclusions derived form the experiment are summarized as below:

1.    Pulse on time (Ton) and built up time (τ) along with Voltage dictates the depth of penetration of heat source.

2.    Pulse on time (Ton) is most significant factor.

3.    The thickness of recast layer depends upon current, voltage and cycle time.

4.    The surface roughness increases as pulse on time increases further resulting in higher rate of MRR (at the cost of surface texture obtained).

5.    Taper is observed in the machined part which is a inevitable phenomenon while processing using WEDM (due to erosion of wire).
