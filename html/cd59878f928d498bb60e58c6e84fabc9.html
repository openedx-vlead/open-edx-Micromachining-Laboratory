The conclusions derived form the experiment are summarized as below:

1.    Microstructure analysis reveals that laser hardening of Cast Iron using Nd: YAG laser is quite feasible.

2.    No abnormal metallurgical change is witnessed during the process.

3.    The process parameters like laser power density, spot size, beam energy distribution as well as the thermo physical properties of the material affect the process, independently.

4.    The increase in surface hardness from 240 Hv to 600 to 700 Hv is normally observed. The material removal rate increases with increase in current density.
